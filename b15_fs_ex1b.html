3 best films of all time

Back to the Future

McFly, a 17-year-old high school student,
is accidentally sent 30 years into the past in a time-traveling DeLorean invented by his close friend,
the maverick scientist Doc Brown.

Indiana Jones

1936, archaeologist and adventurer Indiana Jones is hired by the U.S. government to find the Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis can obtain its awesome powers.

Star Wars

Jedi escape a hostile blockade to find allies and come across a young boy who may bring balance to the Force, but the long dormant Sith resurface to claim their original glory.
